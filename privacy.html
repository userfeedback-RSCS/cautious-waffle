Privacy Policy for this music identity MVP

Last updated: November 17, 2025

This is an early-stage project exploring how to move your music “identity” between streaming services. We collect only the information we need to run the waitlist, learn from user feedback, and improve the product.

Information We Collect

  • Email address — when you join the waitlist or complete the survey.
  • Survey responses — if you choose to provide them.
  • IP address & timestamp — recorded at the moment you submit your email, for consent verification and anti-spam protection.
  • Technical logs — basic, non-identifying data from our hosting provider (like page views).

How We Use Your Information

  • Provide early access and updates about this project.
  • Invite you to optional user interviews.
  • Improve the product based on aggregated feedback.
  • Verify consent and protect against spam or abuse.

We do not sell or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

How We Store Your Information

Your information is stored securely in Notion, Tally, and our hosting provider’s systems.
